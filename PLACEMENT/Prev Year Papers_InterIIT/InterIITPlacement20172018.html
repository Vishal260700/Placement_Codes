Here, Consider blank as integer (n+1).

We need to find length of cycles in permutation of numbers. But the constraint is we can swap with the blank only.  

First introduce blank(n+1) in a cycle by swapping with a number of the cycle and then find length.

Answer will be summation of (cycle length - 1) for all cycle.

Example

2 1 4 3 _ (Given)

2 1 4 3 5 (Consider)

5 1 4 3 2 (Introducing blank in cycle of 1 and 2. Now we have cycle of 1 2 5 ) (Ans = 1 swap)

5 1 4 3 2 (cycle of length 3. Ans = 1 + 2 = 3)

1 2 4 3 5

1 2 5 3 4 (Introducing blank in cycle of 3 and 4. Now we have cycle of 3 4 5 ) (Ans = 3 + 1 = 4)

1 2 5 3 4 (cycle of length 3. Ans = 4 + 2 = 6)

1 2 3 4 5

Answer = 6
